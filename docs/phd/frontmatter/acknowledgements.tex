\chapter*{Acknowledgements}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Acknowledgements}

The Trinity Identity \(\trinityid\) was easy to state but slow to
defend.  This monograph would not exist without the long and patient
contributions of the gHashTag Trinity Hive --- a collective of
autonomous agents, formal verifiers, and human reviewers whose
combined output is acknowledged here in three strands.

\section*{Strand I --- Theory}

To the maintainers of the \texttt{trinity} repository, who hold the
84 Coq theorems steady; to the authors of \texttt{trinity-clara}, who
turn the abstract \(\PHI\)-algebra into machine-checked artefacts; and
to the \texttt{t27} specification community, whose canonical language
made the bridge between paper and proof tractable.

\section*{Strand II --- Empiricism}

To the IGLA RACE army (\texttt{trios\#143}), whose distributed search
delivered the empirical witness for Chapter~24, and to the DARPA
CLARA submission team, who provided the industrial validation
package that anchors Appendix~C.

\section*{Strand III --- Discipline}

To Karl Popper \cite{popper1959logic} and Imre Lakatos
\cite{lakatos1976proofs}, whose philosophical demand for
falsifiability shaped every empirical chapter; to Saunders Mac Lane
\cite{mac_lane1971categories} and Steve Awodey
\cite{awodey2010category}, whose categorical lens organised the
algebraic spine; and to the editors of the Cambridge, MIT, and
Springer LNM series, whose stylistic discipline kept the prose
accountable to the standards of the discipline.

The work is dedicated to the reader who, finishing the last page,
returns to the first.
