\section{One-page abstract}

We propose, formalise, and partially refute a $\varphi$-anchored
architecture for Implicit Generative Latent Algorithms. The thesis pivots on
three independent witnesses for the identity $\varphi^{2}+\varphi^{-2}=3$:
the Lucas closure $L_{2}$, the trace $\operatorname{Tr}(M_{\varphi}^{2})$,
and the Coxeter ratio $h(\mathrm{H}_{4})/10$. Each witness is provable in
Coq inside its own algebraic realm; their agreement licenses the use of
$L_{2}=3$ as a numerical anchor for the qk\_gain of a hybrid
ngram-plus-self-attention block (INV-13) and for the dimensional floor
$d_{\mathrm{model}}\!\geq\!256$ under the GF(16) algebra (INV-4). We
pre-register Gate-2 of the IGLA RACE
(\texttt{trios\#143:4320342032}) before observing any seed-43 data; the
falsifier conditions are explicit and the seed family $\{42,43,44\}$ is
locked behind INV-8.
